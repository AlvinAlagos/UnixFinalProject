Welcome to Healthy Regime

Healthy Regime is a website containing basic information for newcomers into the fitness world. Our goal here is to make sure our valued customers will be able to start their new journey with HealthyRegime, guiding them to a successful healthy life.

Workouts

You will find a list of workouts on here, with that list you'll be able to make your first workout routine that you feel will match you. Of course, we took into accommodation for begginners and listed basic compound movements that will help ease your introduction to working out. You will find that list on the workout tab.

Diet

Healthy Regime will also be providing our visitors a guide to building a healthy meal plan. When it comes to working out, eating correct is as much important as keeping a consistent workout routine. You will find this guide on the Diet Page.

Coaches

We will also supply our customers with assistance from 4 of our personal coaches here at Healthy Regime. We made sure that all 4 of these coaches are equipped experience in fitness. Each of these coaches specialize in a specific field in the world of fitness. To learn more about our coaches, visit the coaches and you will find more information about each and of them, also feel free to contact them for consulting!

BMI

The Healthy Regime website also gives our online visitors to oppurtunity to calculate their BMI (Body Mass Index). Calculating your body mass index gives you an idea of where you stand with your weight and height. With gaining the knowledge of your BMI, you can set your goal in terms of workin out and eating right, either to bulk or to cut. So go ahead, feel free to use our BMI calculator!
