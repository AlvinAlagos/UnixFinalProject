Body Mass Index

Knowing your body mass index will inform you of where you stand in terms of your weight and your height. On the right hand side you will a chart representing a BMI chart for every height and weight category. Once you attain your BMI see on the chart where you stand as of now, you will fall upon either underweight, healthy, overweight, obese or extremely obese.
